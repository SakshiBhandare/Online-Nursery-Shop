Welcome to Ugaoo Nursery, India's largest and most trusted online plant nursery, where nature's beauty meets your doorstep. We are a passionate team of plant enthusiasts and gardening experts dedicated to bringing the transformative power of greenery into your homes and lives. Ugaoo Nursery was born out of a simple yet profound belief: that plants have the power to enrich our lives in countless ways. We believe that plants are not just decorative elements; they are living companions that bring joy, tranquility, and a touch of nature into our homes. Founded in 2016, Ugaoo Nursery has quickly become a leading force in the Indian online plant nursery industry. We have grown from a small startup to a thriving enterprise, serving over a million satisfied customers across India. Our mission is simple yet ambitious: to make gardening accessible and enjoyable for everyone. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to experience the joy of nurturing a plant, watching it grow, and reaping the rewards of a green and healthy home.


We invite you to join us on your gardening journey. Whether you are a seasoned plant enthusiast or a complete beginner, we have something for everyone. Visit our online store to browse our extensive collection of plants and gardening accessories. We also have a wealth of information available on our website and social media channels to help you learn more about plant care and gardening. Thank you for choosing Ugaoo Nursery. We look forward to helping you create a beautiful and thriving garden in your home.
